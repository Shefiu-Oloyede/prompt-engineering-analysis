# Key Findings & Recommendations

## Executive Summary

Tested 5 distinct prompt engineering approaches on ChatGPT. 
**Result:** Structured, detailed prompts significantly outperform simple requests 
(4.8/5 vs 4.0/5 average score).

## Winner: Detailed & Structured Prompts

**Score: 4.8/5** ⭐

### Why It Works:
- Clear formatting requirements
- Specific output structure
- Well-defined expectations
- Professional, consistent results

### Example:
### Result:
- All requirements met
- Professional tone maintained
- Highly usable output
- Only minor issue: slightly exceeded word count (acceptable)

---

## Runner-Up: Chain-of-Thought Prompts

**Score: 4.6/5** 🥈

### Why It Works:
- Forces step-by-step reasoning
- More thoughtful responses
- Useful for complex problems
- Shows "thinking process"

### Best For:
- Analysis tasks
- Problem-solving
- Complex decision-making
- Educational content

---

## Takeaway

**The more specific your prompt, the better your result.**

Use **detailed, structured prompts** for consistent, professional outputs.
Use **chain-of-thought prompts** for complex reasoning tasks.
Avoid simple, vague prompts unless you're testing baseline performance.
